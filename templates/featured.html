Like No Udder

Like No Udder is not only about making history (we're the world's first all vegan soft serve ice cream truck). We're also about serving delicious products that just happen to be free of all animal products. This includes dairy, eggs, animal fats and honey. Our mission is to bring smiles to peoples' faces and serve the best possible dairy-free treats available. Years of research have culminated into this dream and we hope you have a chance to share in the joy that is vegan soft serve.

My first vegan business, The Screaming Vegan, was a wholesale baking business where I diligently made cupcakes, cakes, cookies and brownies. While I loved providing a product for my loyal customers, baker's hours were not my cup of tea. So I left that world behind, but not without taking valuable business lessons and food passions along with me.

In addition to non-dairy ice cream products, Like No Udder currently serves candy bars and meatless jerky, and during day long festivals may offer savory items like vegan meatball subs and vegan hot dogs. And who knows what might get added to the menu next. Stay tuned to find out when we might be in your neighborhood next and come share a guilt free treat. I thank you and the cows thank you!

~Karen Krinsky, Owner of Like No Udder
